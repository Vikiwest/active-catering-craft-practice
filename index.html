Author's Biography

OLORUNDA THERESA ONYEJE

Born in the 'sixties, Theresa had her primary education in Lagos premier day school, Surulere Lagos, then proceeded to Maryland Comprehensive Sec Sch Ikeja, Lagos for her secondary education. Olorunda Theresa is a graduate of university of Nigeria Nsukka, college of Education Eha Amufu & IMT Enugu. An experienced teacher of Foods & Nutrition, Home Management & Catering Craft in Command Secondary School Abakaliki & Ikeja for the past 37 years. She has worked in Hotel presidential Enugu, & taught catering in vocational institutions before it was introduced into secondary schools in the federation. Having participated in a number of skill acquisition programmes, due to her inborn passion for Vocational Education, she has mentored many individuals in Catering craft which are entrepreneurs having their personal businesses today. Theresa Olorunda is one of the catering teachers, the Subject head and Hod Technology junior school in Command day Secondary school Ikeja Lagos. She is an Assistant Director of Education with Ministry of Defence.
