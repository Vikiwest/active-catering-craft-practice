About Our Books

Introducing "Active Catering Craft Practice"for Senior Secondary Ss1 - Ss3, Technical & Vocational Colleges. It is a "4 in 1" Textbook comprising Theory, Workbook, Past Exam Questions & Answers, Easy & Seperate Recipes on Appertizer, Dessert, African dishes, Snacks & other recipes with ingredients available in our locality. The book contains Topics like Entrepreneurship, Service convention, Food costing etc, all broken down for easy understanding. Active Catering Craft Books are practical catering books for all senior secondary school students who have taken up catering craft as a trade subject in school, for teachers looking for quality catering materials to teach their students. It also has some Standard Waec & Neco questions and answers for students to practice with. Teachers who have absolutely no knowledge of catering, go through this Text book & become well equipped to teach the subject. There are lots of Topics to be explored inside this text book, for those looking for rudimentary/basic knowledge, average knowledge, or advanced knowledge on catering craft, then this text book is highly recommended for you. Workbooks: The Active Catering workbook are filled with Waec/Neco Standard questions for Ss1-Ss3 Students. Past question & Answer compilation is also available in Catering Craft & in Foods & Nutrition. The author of the Active Catering Books is a well experienced and highly skilled caterer and Food Nutritionist with more than 35 years experience in Catering, having taught so many students within that span. She is the subject head of Catering & Hod Technology junior school in Command Day Secondary School Ikeja, Lagos State till date.
